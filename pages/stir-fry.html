Stir-Fry is an amazing dish from asian cuisine that is as simple as it is delicious. Combining delicious quickly fried meat with vegetables pasta or rice and amazing sauce to create fulfilling dish that will fill you for hours and with little cleanup as it will be one pot(or wok) dish!

  • 200g rice pasta
  • 6 small spring onion
  • 100g shiitake mushrooms
  • 2 tsp grated ginger
  • 4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • peanut oil

for marinade you will need

  • 250g beef
  • 1 tbsp peanut oil
  • 1 tbsp dark soy sauce
  • 1 tsp cornstarch

for the sauce you need

  • 5 tsp dark soy sauce
  • 2 tbsp oyster sauce
  • 3 tsp mirin
  • 2 tsp sesame oil
  • 1 tsp brown sugar
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp white pepper

Steps

    Marinade:

  1. Cut beef into as thin slices as possible, against the fibers
  2. Mix peanut oil cornstarch and soy sauce with the cut beef and leave for 15 minutes.
  3. While the meat is marinading, boil pasta in accordance with package instructions
  4. Slice shiitake mushrooms thinly, grate the ginger, and mince garlic
  5. Cut spring onions into 5cm chunks
  6. Mix all of the ingredients in small bowl to create sauce

Dish

  1. Heat 3 tbsp of peanut oil on wok on high heat,until it starts to smoke
  2. Add marinated beef, and cook for 2/2,5 minutes, then take it out of the wok
  3. Heat 3 tbsp of peanut oil on wok, and add thinly sliced shiitake mushrooms
  4. After 1/2 minutes add grated ginger and minced garlic and cook while stirring for another minute
  5. Add beef back into the wok
  6. Add sauce and spring onions
  7. Add boiled pasta
  8. Mix everything together and serve immiedietly
