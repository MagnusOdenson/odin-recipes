Tomato Sauce

The 3 ingredient tomato sauce is as simple as it is delicious. Popularized by the great Marcella Hazan it's a great way to create quick, easy, and absolutely the simplest sauce that I can imagine.

All you need is:

  • 42g or 5 tbps of butter
  • 2 cans of whole, peeled tomato's
  • 1 onion, sliced into fourths, peeled

Steps

  1. Place the butter, tomato's, and onion in the saucepan
  2. Set temp to medium high
  3. Let sauce heat for 20 to 25 minutes, ocasionally stirring
  4. Remove cooked onion (it can be used as topping or just discarded)
